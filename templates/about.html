About ragchat

What is ragchat?

Upload Your Documents

Create your own knowledge domains by uploading your documents. ragchat supports various document formats and organizes them into intuitive domain structures for easy access and management.

Chat with Information

Interact with your documents through natural conversation. Ask questions, get insights, and receive accurate information extracted directly from your uploaded content.

Get Resources

Every answer comes with source validation. See exactly where information comes from with direct references to your documents, ensuring reliability and traceability.

Cross File Analysis

Connect information across multiple documents. ragchat analyzes relationships between different files, helping you discover insights that might be missed in isolated readings.
