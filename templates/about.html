What is ragchat?

Upload Documents

Upload your documents and transform them into an AI-powered intelligent library. Store up to 100MB of documents within your specialized domain folders. Currently supporting PDF, DOCX and TXT files.

Chat to Get Information

Chat with your documents through AI-powered natural conversations. Ask questions in everyday language and get instant, accurate answers extracted directly from your specialized document storage.

Source Validation

Get precise source validation for every answer. See exactly where information comes from with page numbers and direct references from your PDFs and documents. Validate every step you take, save time and energy, and increase efficiency.

Connected Documents

Analyze relationships across multiple documents. Make AI-powered analysis to discover hidden connections between your files. Save hours of time from manual cross-referencing, get deeper information, and make better decisions.
